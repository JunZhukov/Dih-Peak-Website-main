This website was developed as part of our project for the Practical Research 2 (PR2) IT class. It serves as a comprehensive compilation of frequently asked questions (FAQs) specifically related to HTML5 and CSS3, two foundational technologies we extensively studied throughout the course. The purpose of this project is to showcase our understanding and proficiency in front-end web development by applying key concepts, including HTML5 structure, semantic elements, and CSS3 styling techniques, to create a fully functional and visually appealing website. Through this project, we aimed to not only enhance our technical skills but also demonstrate our ability to organize information in a clear and user-friendly format, making it easy for users to find answers to common web development questions. This website highlights the practical application of the knowledge we gained and is a testament to our growth as developers in this rapidly evolving field.
